About Us

We can develop devices spanning a large area of applicability. Our solutions are custom tailored to our clients' needs.

We create custom devices that can be equipped with graphical user interfaces, radio modules (wifi / long range), dedicated PC software.

Our team is in close collaboration with Labio a.s. from Czechia in the fileds of gas chromatography and spectrometry. For our partners, we have developed:
• Electronic measurement and control systems for temperature and pressure.
• Data acquisition systems
• Motorized mechanical assemblies
• Chromatography related devices
• Software interfaces for devices
• Experimental analyses of processes
• Various mechanical components

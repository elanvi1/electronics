IOT Wireless Sensor Network

We designed a radio solution for equipment interconnectivity over large distances. It is applicable to sensors, motors, other actuators, etc.
Low power mode is possible, working on batteries. Thus, the sensors can be periodically querried at intervals ranging from one second to several hours, or they can be triggered by an external interrupt.

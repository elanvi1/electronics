Proiectări Cuptoare Micluț

Founded in 1990, our company operates in the industrial furnace area. Our main activity focuses on designing, construction, upgrading and rapairing of any type of industrial furnace.

As a secondary activity, we have developed in the area of electronics. Our capabilities include:

  • Electronic circuits design
  • Prototypes and small series manufacturing and assembly
  • Industrial automation
  • Control software development
  • RGB light shows
  • Laboratory equipment
  • Gas chromatography
  • Wireless sensor networks
  • Servicing
  • Data processing software, machine learning
  • Custom user interfaces, touchscreen HMIs
